What means quality inspection

  • Check a product meets some criteria
  • Applies to bought, produced and sold products
  • Related to a product or a lot of products
  • ISO Standards

Requirements

  • Simplicity
  • Flexibility
  • Powerful system without programming
  • Flexibility
  • Did I mention flexibility?

Use cases

  • Mainly related to fruits and vegetables
  • Different results should be computed from quality inputs
  • Quality results used to compute invoice price

Approach

  • Quality tests defined for each product
  • Inputs is dictionary field
  • Result is a dictionary computed field
  • A formula is used to define the results
  • Origin values can be used for the formula

Results

  • Quality administration have full control
  • User only needs to focus on inputs
